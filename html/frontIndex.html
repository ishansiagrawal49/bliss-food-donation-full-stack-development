ABOUT US

We are helping hands of those organisations which are fighting to provide meals to individuals who are unable to purchase or prepare their own meals. We have a vision through which we not only make food available to the poor but also save wastage of excess food. Our goal is to redistribute from those who have excess of it which otherwise will be wasted to those who are fighting for their meals. In this age of computers we want to provide the NGOs a technological platform to make the process a little easier.



DONATE

We provide an easier platform for people to donate excess food their nearest food charity.So if you own a restaurant or have a party at your home,you can do your bit by giving away the excess food.You don't loose anything,do you? But the satisfaction of contributing towards society is unparalleled.

OUR FAMILY

Our family consists all those people who are with us in the fight to make India a hunger free country. You can be a part of our family by just contributing to our fight with hunger. Just choose a NGO nerby and make a contribution. Even a small contribution counts, and congratulations you are now a part of our family. If you are a NGO just go to the register page and get registered to be a part of our family, we will then reach you out with contributions from our users.
